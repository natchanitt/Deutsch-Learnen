About Deutsch Lernen

Helping students speak German with confidence since 2016.

Our Story

A school built around how people actually learn languages

Deutsch Lernen started as a handful of evening classes for friends preparing to study and work in Germany. Today we run structured A1–C1 courses, but the goal hasn't changed: get learners speaking real German as quickly and confidently as possible, with small classes and teachers who give every student personal attention.
