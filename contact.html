FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any German knowledge to start?

No — the A1 Beginner course assumes no prior knowledge at all.

Can I switch levels after booking?

Yes, contact us within the first week of class and we'll move you to a better-fitting level at no extra cost.

Are classes online or in person?

Both — choose the format that suits you when you book your class.

When do I pay for my course?

Payment is collected on your first day of class; booking online simply reserves your seat.
